# Product Overview: SmartExpense AI

**Purpose:** 
SmartExpense AI is an enterprise-grade expense management system that automates the extraction, validation, and approval of employee expense reports using IBM watsonx Orchestrate. It eliminates manual data entry by parsing receipts and invoices automatically.

**Target Users:** 
1. **Employees:** Who upload receipts and submit expense claims.
2. **Finance Approvers:** Who review flagged or high-value expenses.
3. **System Administrators:** Who configure approval routing rules and manage integrations.

**Key Features:**
- Automated OCR and data extraction from PDF/Image receipts.
- Real-time policy validation (e.g., flagging meals over $100).
- Automated approval routing based on management hierarchy.
- Export of approved expenses to SAP/Oracle ERP systems.

**Business Objectives:**
- Reduce manual expense processing time by 80%.
- Decrease policy violation errors to under 1%.
- Ensure 100% auditability of all automated decisions.
